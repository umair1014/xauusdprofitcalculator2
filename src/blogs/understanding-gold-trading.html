Spot Gold Trading

Spot trading involves buying or selling gold at the current market price. This method offers:

  • Immediate execution
  • Direct market access
  • Transparent pricing
  • Lower counterparty risk

Gold Futures Trading

Futures contracts allow you to trade gold at a predetermined future price:

  • Standardized contracts
  • Higher leverage options
  • Regulated exchanges
  • Good for hedging

FAQs

What's the best time to trade gold?

The most active trading hours are during London and New York sessions (8:00-16:00 GMT) when liquidity is highest.

How much capital do I need to start trading gold?

While you can start with as little as $100 on some platforms, a recommended minimum is $1,000 to allow for proper position sizing and risk management.

Is gold trading suitable for beginners?

Yes, but start with education and practice on a demo account. Understanding the market and risk management is crucial before trading with real money.
