Understanding XAUUSD Basics

At its core, XAUUSD represents the price of gold quoted in US dollars. "XAU" is the international code for one troy ounce of gold, while "USD" stands for the US dollar. When you trade XAUUSD in the forex market, you're essentially buying or selling gold against the dollar.

Why Is XAUUSD Important in Forex?

Gold has been valuable for thousands of years, but in forex trading, its value goes beyond history:

  • Safe Haven in Turbulence: When economies falter or inflation spikes, traders often move to gold.
  • Inverse USD Relationship: When the US dollar weakens, gold often gains value, and vice versa.
  • Inflation Hedge: Gold tends to maintain value even as currencies lose purchasing power.
